Home


The Home page serves as the central hub for users to input their text based on provided prompts. Users can also opt for a random prompt by clicking "Give Prompt." Upon submission, users are redirected to the Results page, where they can view their predicted personality type alongside a visual chart depicting emotion and sentiment.


Explore


The Explore page enables users to delve into the dataset used for personality prediction. Divided into two sections—general overview and detailed personality—users can navigate through the data, gaining insights into the patterns and nuances that contribute to our prediction model.


Methodology


Visit the Methodology page to gain a deeper understanding of the methods employed in developing our predictive model. This section provides transparency, allowing users to comprehend the intricacies and processes involved in the modeling of Myers-Briggs personality types.


About


The About page offers users background information on the research topic—MBTI personality prediction. Discover the motivation behind the project, the team's dedication, and the significance of predicting personality types through machine learning and data analysis.
